About Us

Yes Ma'am is an online data insights platform for schools and educational institutes. We provide the schools with a dashboard and an app to monitor attendance, results, communicate with parents. Education is the process of facilitating learning, or the acquisition of knowledge, skills, values, beliefs, and habits. Educational methods include storytelling, discussion, teaching, training, and directed research. Yes Ma'am is a smart way to analyze performance of school in education matter.
